About Kalachuchi

Kalachuchi is a vintage haven nestled in Palo, Leyte, born from the passion of its owner, Lica Yzabelle De Veyra. Lica's love for vintage fashion began years ago, fueled by a desire to unearth timeless treasures and give them a second life. In 2022, she decided to share her passion with the world, opening Kalachuchi as a place where vintage lovers could discover unique finds and add a touch of charm to their style.

At Kalachuchi, vintage is more than fashion – it's about embracing a slower pace, appreciating the craftsmanship of the past, and celebrating individuality. Our curated collection includes pre-loved clothing, handbags, accessories, and home décor – each chosen for its quality, style, and the story it tells.

Kalachuchi is also deeply committed to giving back. A portion of our profits supports local cat and dog rescue organizations, helping provide care and find loving homes for animals in need.

Whether you're searching for a statement handbag, a unique outfit, or a piece to add soul to your space, we invite you to explore our collection. We’re thrilled to help you find your next vintage favorite!

We proudly serve the vibrant community of vintage lovers in Tacloban and nearby areas, offering local meetups and deliveries for your convenience.

We hope you enjoy your Kalachuchi experience!
