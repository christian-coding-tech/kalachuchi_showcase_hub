How to Order from Kalachuchi

  1. Browse Our Catalog: Explore our collection of vintage treasures! Click on the items you'd like to purchase.
  2. Check Out: Once you've chosen your items, simply click "Check Out."
  3. Direct Link to Seller: You'll be directly linked to our seller (that’s me!), where you’ll receive instructions on how to make your payment.
  4. Payment: Pay using GCash or GoTyme as instructed.
  5. Shipping Information: Once payment is confirmed, we’ll ask you to provide your full name, full address, contact number, and helpful landmarks for smooth delivery.
  6. Shipping: We’ll ship your items via Flash Express (nationwide) or Maxim (Tacloban & Palo).
  7. Tracking Code: You’ll receive a Messenger message with a tracking code once your order is shipped.
